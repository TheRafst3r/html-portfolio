The Best Movies of 2024

Rafa Medina's Top 3 Movies of 2024

Dune Part 2

The best sci-fi movie I have seen in a long time. The effects are amazing

Wicked Part 1

I am not a fan of musicals but I was impressed by this one. The movie had a lot of good songs

Nosferatu

The movie was not as scary as I hoped but the story and performances were great
